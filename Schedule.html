ACADEMY OF SELF DEFENSE SCHEDULE OF CLASSES

We are running in-person classes, zoom interactive classes, and online streaming classes!

In-Person Classes (Santa Clara,Ca)

In-person classes run Monday-Sunday, as early as 6:30am and as late as 8pm! We offer a variety of in-person training: Krav Maga (Self Defense), Muay Thai Kickboxing (Traditional and Dutch Style), Modern Combat Hapkido, Muay Thai HIIT, Boot Camp, ASD Fighter Fit, Kids Krav Maga, and Fitness Unlimited Classes!

Live Zoom Interactive Classes

For those who can't make it into our local studio, we are still running live interactive Zoom classes during the week! You can join us from anywhere in the world and learn Krav Maga (Self-Defense) for both adults & kids and Muay Thai Kickboxing. You can even join us for some Boot Camp & ASD Fighter Fit Classes as well!

Online Streaming Classes

You can not join us virtually from anywhere in the world. Stream classes right from our website (no Zoom or camera required). No matter what time zone you are in, there will be a streaming class that will fit your schedule! We are streaming all of our programs: Krav Maga (Adults & Kids), Muay Thai Kickboxing, JCF Cardio Kickboxing, Boot Camp, and ASD Fighter Fit!

Events & Seminars

In addition to our classes, we also host various specialty events and seminars. These range from Women's Self Defense, Master Classes, Kettlebell Seminars, Car Jacking Seminars, Weapons Seminars, and More.
